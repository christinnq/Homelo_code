About HOMELO

Your trusted partner in finding the perfect home with over 15 years of excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2009, HOMELO has been at the forefront of Romania's real estate revolution. We started with a simple mission: to make finding your dream home as easy and enjoyable as possible.

With over 15 years in the real estate market, we have built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and outstanding customer service. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to making your property journey as smooth and successful as possible.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 500 families find their perfect homes, with a 98% customer satisfaction rate that speaks to our commitment to excellence.
